About Him


Sheikh Fareed was a beloved scholar and a teacher whose life was dedicated to the teachings of the Qur'an, sunnah and the service of the ummah.

This website stands as a tribute to his legacy - a place where his voice continues to echo, and his knowledge remains a source of guidance for generations to come. It is a humble effort to preserve his light and teachings so they may continue to benefit others, even after his return to Allah. May everyone who benefits from his recitations or teachings be a means of continuous reward for him in the hereafter.

May Allah have mercy on him, forgive his shortcomings and grant him the highest levels of jannah, al-Firdaws. May his legacy continue to be a source of guidance and reward. Aameen.
